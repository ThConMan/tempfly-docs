TempFly

Temporary flight that only burns time while you're actually airborne. Hand out Fly Vouchers and stop paying for time spent standing still.

Paper 26.1+ Java 21+ Premium · closed source
Get it Documentation

The idea

Give players temporary flight without paying for time they spend parked.

Most "temp fly" setups bleed time the whole time it's on — even while a player stands in their base sorting chests. TempFly only counts down while you're actually in the air. Land or stand still and the timer pauses. Time is granted with Fly Vouchers — feather items players right-click to top up their balance — so flight becomes a real, tradeable reward instead of a flat permission.

How it works

Pay for air time, not idle time.

Airborne-only drain

Time counts down only while you're flying. Land or stand still and the timer pauses instantly — no more bleeding minutes parked in your base.

Live countdown

A live action-bar countdown shows exactly how much flight time you have left, shifting green → yellow → red as it runs low so a landing is never a surprise.

Fly Vouchers

Hand out time as feather items (fully configurable) that grant flight on right-click. Each voucher is HMAC-signed, so it can't be forged, duped, or hand-crafted.

Persistence

Balances are stored in SQLite and survive restarts. Log off mid-air and your flight is restored right where you left it the next time you join.

Fall grace

When flight runs out, you get a few seconds of fall-damage immunity to glide down safely — no splatting the moment your time expires.

WorldGuard regions

A temp-fly region flag lets you forbid temporary flight inside specific regions — spawn, arenas, events — while players keep their banked time for everywhere else.

How it plays out

A simple loop that rewards flying, not idling.

1

Earn a voucher

Pick up a Fly Voucher from a crate, shop, reward, or staff hand-out and right-click it to bank the time.

2

Take off

Run /tempfly to toggle flight. The action bar shows your remaining time, counting down only while you're airborne.

3

Land & bank the rest

Touch down and the timer pauses. Your leftover time is saved for next time — and you glide safely if it ever runs out mid-air.

Plays nice with your stack

Optional hooks — TempFly runs fine on a bare server too.

WorldGuard
Adds a temp-fly region flag to forbid flight in chosen regions.
SQLite
Built-in, file-based storage — no database server to run.
Any crate / reward plugin
Hand out Fly Vouchers via /tempfly give from console.

Drop it in and take off

Sensible defaults out of the box — vouchers, drain, and regions all configurable.
